About the Founder

Monica Furdui, based in Vermont, USA, is the visionary behind My Goddess Project — a movement helping women reconnect with their strength, elegance, and inner confidence. With a background in artificial intelligence and human transformation, Monica blends science, mindset, and style into a holistic system for lasting change.

What began as her own 6-week transformation has grown into a global program empowering women to embody their most radiant, grounded, and feminine selves — from the heart of Vermont to wherever you are.
