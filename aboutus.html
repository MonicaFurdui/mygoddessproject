Monica Furdui, based in beautiful Vermont, USA, is the visionary behind My Goddess Project — a movement helping women reconnect with their strength, elegance, and inner confidence. With a background in artificial intelligence and human transformation, Monica combines science, mindset, and style to create a holistic system for lasting change. What began as her own 6-week transformation journey has evolved into a program that empowers women worldwide to embody their most radiant, grounded, and feminine selves
